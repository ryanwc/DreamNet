* mouse over the colored icons below for examples of each kind of tag
Dream tags
typeA "type" tag indicates an important theme that describes the dream. Examples include "flying", "nightmare", "futuristic", "missed/late to appointment/event", and "past".
personA "person" tag identifies a person who appeared in the dream. Examples include "mother", "son-in-law", "boss/manager", "best friend", and "person from childhood". (Note: No real names!)
placeA "place" tag identifies a place from the dream. Examples include "kitchen", "foreign country", "house", "forest", and "alien world"
thingA "thing" tag identifies an object or creature that appeared in the dream. Examples include "bowl", "door", "hat", "ghost", and "caterpillar".
emotionAn "emotion" tag identifies an emotion felt during the dream. Examples include "happiness", "terror", "courage", "anxiety", and "love".
sensationA "sensation" tag identifies a physical sensation perceived during the dream. Examples include "pain", "pleasure", "cold", "blue", and "sour".
